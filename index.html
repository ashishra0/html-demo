A modern solution to an old age problem.

At Sahayata Sticks we are redesigning and developing a smart walking cane for the elderly which will enable senior citizens to be independent while also giving a sense of security to their loved ones.

Our mission is to mitigate the challenges faced by the golden-agers in their day to day life and make sure our product should be accessible to every section of our society. We aim to integrate the modern technology to find innovative ways and enhance traditional walking sticks. The stick would be user friendly and comprises of straightforward functions.
